Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Can the receptionist book appointments? ＋

Yes. It can check available times in your connected calendar and confirm suitable appointments with callers.

Can it answer questions about my business? ＋

Yes. We configure it using your opening hours, services, locations, common questions and preferred call-handling process.

What happens if I use all my included minutes? ＋

Additional usage is billed at £0.50 per minute so calls are not immediately interrupted, although overage is intended for occasional busy periods rather than regular use.

Is there a long-term contract? ＋

No. Our plans are designed to remain flexible, and you can cancel your subscription when needed.

How long does setup take? ＋

Setup time depends on the complexity of your call flow and integrations. We will explain the expected process during your demonstration.

Can urgent calls be transferred? ＋

Yes. We can create rules for urgent enquiries and decide when a caller should be transferred or when your team should be notified.

What happens if I regularly exceed my included minutes?

Occasional busy months are fine, but extra usage is charged at £0.50 per minute and is designed to make upgrading the better-value option for consistently higher usage. If your usage repeatedly sits well above your plan allowance, we may contact you to recommend a more suitable package from your next billing period. We will always discuss this with you before making any change.
