Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

This policy explains how We Call Flow uses cookies and similar technologies on its website.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites function, remember preferences and understand how visitors use a site.

2. Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for core website functions, security and consent preferences. They do not require consent where strictly necessary.

3. Analytics cookies

We may use analytics tools to understand visits, page performance and user journeys. These cookies will not be activated until you give consent where required.

4. Marketing cookies

If we introduce advertising or remarketing tools, they may use cookies to measure campaigns and personalise advertising. These will require consent where applicable.

5. Managing your choices

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies through our cookie banner when it is enabled. You can also delete or block cookies using your browser settings, although this may affect some functions.

6. Current website position

Until analytics or advertising tools are connected, the website should use only essential functionality. This policy must be updated when new providers are introduced.
